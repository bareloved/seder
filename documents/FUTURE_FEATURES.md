## Priority 1 - High Impact

### Expenses Tracking (הוצאות)
- [ ] Expense entries with categories (equipment, travel, software, etc.)
- [ ] Receipt photo upload and OCR
- [ ] Recurring expenses (monthly subscriptions)
- [ ] Profit/loss reports combining income and expenses
- [ ] VAT deduction tracking for tax purposes

### Invoicing System (חשבוניות)
- [ ] Generate invoices directly from income entries
- [ ] Customizable invoice templates with logo
- [ ] PDF export and email sending
- [ ] Invoice numbering sequence
- [ ] Payment reminders for overdue invoices
- [ ] Integration with Israeli invoice services (Green Invoice, Meshulam)

### Client Management (ניהול לקוחות)
- [ ] Client database with contact info
- [ ] Client-specific rates and terms
- [ ] Client income history and analytics
- [ ] Automatic client name suggestions based on history
- [ ] Client notes and communication log
