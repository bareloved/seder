## 4. תיאור באנגלית (לחנות App Store בינלאומית)

### App Name
**Seder — Freelancer Income Tracker**

### Subtitle
**Track work, invoices & payments**

### Description

Seder is the simple, smart way for freelancers to manage their income.

Track work, invoices, and payments in one place. Built for freelancers who want clarity on their cashflow without the complexity.

**Key features:**
- Google Calendar import — gigs, lessons, and meetings added automatically
- Invoice tracking — always know what's been sent and what hasn't
- Payment status — draft, sent, paid — everything at a glance
- Monthly overview — earnings, trends, and where your money is
- Works on web and iPhone
- Full privacy — no ads, no data selling

**Who is this for?**
Musicians, private tutors, designers, developers — any freelancer who wants to know exactly what they're owed.

**Completely free.** No trial period, no limits.
