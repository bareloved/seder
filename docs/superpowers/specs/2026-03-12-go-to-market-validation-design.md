# Go-to-Market: First 20 Users Validation Plan

## Goal

Find 10-20 real freelancer users for Seder to validate product-market fit before investing in broader marketing. Learn who wants this most and what's missing.

## Target Audience (to be validated)

Israeli freelancers — starting broad, narrowing based on who responds best. Potential segments: musicians/performers, private tutors, designers, developers, therapists, anyone with a תיק עוסק.

## Phase 1: Personal Asks (Weeks 1-2)

Reach out 1-on-1 to 5-10 freelancers you already know.

**How:**
- Personal WhatsApp/text message, not a broadcast
- Frame as asking for help, not pitching: "אני בונה כלי לניהול הכנסות לפרילנסרים, אשמח שתנסה ותגיד לי מה חושב/ת"
- Offer to walk them through it if needed
- After they've used it, ask: what's confusing? What's missing? Would you keep using it?
- If they like it, ask: "מכיר/ה עוד מישהו שזה יכול להתאים לו?"

**Success criteria:**
- 5+ people actually sign up and enter real data
- At least 2-3 return after the first session
- You identify 1-2 patterns in feedback (common pain point, missing feature, confusing flow)

## Phase 2: Community Presence (Weeks 2-4, parallel)

Join 2-3 Israeli freelancer communities and become a helpful presence.

**Where to look:**
- Facebook groups: "פרילנסרים בישראל", musician/performer groups, עוסקים מורשים groups
- Reddit: r/israel freelancer threads
- Any WhatsApp/Telegram groups you're already in

**How:**
- First 1-2 weeks: just read, like, answer questions about income tracking, VAT, invoicing
- Don't mention Seder yet — build familiarity first
- After establishing some presence, share naturally when relevant: "בניתי כלי שעוזר לי עם זה, מישהו רוצה לנסות?"
- Never spam or hard-sell

**Success criteria:**
- Joined 2+ active communities
- Had genuine interactions before sharing Seder
- 3-5 new users from community outreach

## What You're Trying to Learn

These are the key validation questions:

1. **Who cares most?** Which type of freelancer gets excited vs. shrugs?
2. **What's the killer feature?** Income tracking? Invoice status? VAT calculations? Calendar integration? Analytics?
3. **What's the first barrier?** Where do people get stuck or confused?
4. **Would they pay?** (Don't ask directly yet — observe if they keep using it)
5. **How do they track income today?** Excel? Notes app? Accountant does everything? Nothing?

## What's NOT in Scope

- Paid advertising
- Content marketing / blog posts
- App Store listing optimization
- Social media presence / brand accounts
- Pricing strategy (too early)

These are all valid later — but premature before you have 20 users giving you feedback.

## Metrics to Track

Keep it simple — a note on your phone or a spreadsheet:

| Who | How they found Seder | Signed up? | Used it? | Came back? | Key feedback |
|-----|---------------------|------------|----------|------------|--------------|

## Timeline

- **Week 1-2:** Personal outreach to warm contacts. Goal: 5-10 signups.
- **Week 2-4:** Join communities, start participating. Goal: 3-5 more signups.
- **Week 4:** Review what you've learned. Decide whether to double down on a niche or keep broad.
